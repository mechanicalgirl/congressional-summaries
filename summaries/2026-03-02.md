# Congressional Record Summary: Volume 172, No. 38 (February 26, 2026)

## Senate Session Highlights

On February 26, 2026, the Senate confirmed Ryan McCormack as Under Secretary of Transportation for Policy by a vote of 57-33. The chamber considered multiple legislative items before adjourning at 2:54 p.m. to reconvene Monday, March 2, at 3 p.m. A cloture motion was entered on the motion to proceed to H.R. 6644, the Housing for the 21st Century Act, with a vote scheduled for March 2 at 5:30 p.m.

## Legislative Measures Passed

The Senate passed four significant bills during the session. The Digital Coast Act (S. 2245) improved data acquisition and accessibility for coastal programs while extending the program to 2030. The National STEM Week Act (S. 1070) designates an annual National STEM Week to promote American innovation and STEM education pathways for all students, with the program set to expire after five years.

Two previously blocked infrastructure bills also advanced: H.R. 2815, the Cape Fox Land Entitlement Finalization Act, which completes land entitlement for the Native Village of Saxman in Alaska after five decades, and H.R. 972, the Sloan Canyon Conservation and Lateral Pipeline Act, which facilitates construction of a critical water pipeline serving nearly 3 million Las Vegas residents.

## Senate Resolutions Adopted

The chamber passed six commemorative resolutions. These included designations for Career and Technical Education Month (February 2026), National Elizabeth Peratrovich Day (February 16), and Rare Disease Day (February 28). The Senate also passed resolutions supporting National FFA Week, designating the week of February 23-27 as National Public Schools Week, and a separate resolution commemorating Team USA's ice hockey accomplishments at the 2026 Winter Olympics.
