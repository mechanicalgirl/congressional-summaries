# Congressional Record Summary: February 26, 2026

## Legislative Activity and Confirmations

The Senate confirmed Ryan McCormack of Virginia as Under Secretary of Transportation for Policy by a vote of 57-33. The Senate also considered and passed several bills including the Cape Fox Land Entitlement Finalization Act, the Sloan Canyon Conservation and Lateral Pipeline Act, the Digital Coast Act, and the National STEM Week Act. Four hundred forty-five military nominations were favorably reported by the Armed Services Committee.

## Resolutions and Commemorations

The Senate agreed to six resolutions during the session, including designations for Career and Technical Education Month, National Elizabeth Peratrovich Day (recognizing Alaska Native civil rights), Rare Disease Day, National Public Schools Week, and National FFA Week. A resolution commemorating Team USA's accomplishments in ice hockey at the Olympic Winter Games was also submitted. An additional resolution requesting information on Honduras' human rights practices was introduced.

## Committee Hearings and Business

The Armed Services Committee held hearings on two nominees for Assistant Secretary of Defense positions. The Banking, Housing, and Urban Affairs Committee received testimony on regulatory updates from Federal Reserve and banking officials regarding "rightsizing regulation." The Foreign Relations Committee received a closed briefing on tech competition and critical minerals. The Health, Education, Labor, and Pensions Committee ordered favorable reports on bills regarding STEM education modernization, homeschool recognition as high school graduates, living organ donor protections, and healthcare cybersecurity coordination. The Judiciary Committee and Special Committee on Aging held hearings on FDA bureaucracy issues.

## Senate Cloture Action

The Senate entered a cloture motion on the motion to proceed to the Housing for the 21st Century Act (H.R. 6644), with a vote scheduled for 5:30 p.m. on Monday, March 2, 2026. The cloture motion on the McCormack nomination received 60 affirmative votes, clearing the procedural hurdle.
