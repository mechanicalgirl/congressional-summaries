# Congressional Summary (run on 2026-07-11)

[https://www.congress.gov/congressional-record/volume-172/issue-112/daily-digest](https://www.congress.gov/congressional-record/volume-172/issue-112/daily-digest)

On Thursday, July 9, 2026, both chambers of Congress held brief pro forma sessions before adjourning until Monday, July 13. The Senate convened at 4 p.m. for minimal proceedings and immediately adjourned, while the House met at 3 p.m. and introduced 39 public bills and 3 resolutions along with filing six committee reports addressing ALS therapies, health initiatives, travel improvements, and homeland security. When Congress reconvenes on Monday, the House will meet at 4 p.m. with its legislative program to be announced at that time, while the Senate will reconvene at 3 p.m. to continue consideration of Matthew A. Schwartz's nomination for United States Circuit Judge for the Second Circuit. The Senate also scheduled a confirmation vote on Arthur Roberts Jones for United States District Judge for the Southern District of Texas to take place at 5:30 p.m. Monday.
