# Congressional Record Summary (February 26, 2026)

This daily digest documents legislative proceedings for February 26, 2026, with scheduled activities extending through March 2. Here are the main events:

**Senate Activity (Thursday, February 26):**

The Senate passed several bills including the Cape Fox Land Entitlement Finalization Act, the Sloan Canyon Conservation and Lateral Pipeline Act, and the Digital Coast Act. Six resolutions were agreed to recognizing various observances including National Elizabeth Peratrovich Day, Rare Disease Day, and National FFA Week. The Senate confirmed Ryan McCormack as Under Secretary of Transportation for Policy by a vote of 57-33, with cloture achieved 60-34. Thirty bills and eight resolutions were introduced, and 445 military nominations were favorably reported by the Armed Services Committee.

**House Activity (Thursday, February 26):**

The House met in brief session, introducing 24 public bills (H.R. 7720-7743) and 5 resolutions (H. Res. 1086-1090). The chamber adjourned within minutes with no recorded votes. Representative Kennedy of Utah served as Speaker pro tempore.

**Committee Activities:**

Senate committees held several hearings including the Armed Services Committee on military nominations, the Banking Committee on regulatory rightsizing, and the Health, Education, Labor, and Pensions Committee reviewing education and healthcare legislation. The Special Committee on Aging examined FDA bureaucracy issues affecting drug approval processes.

**Upcoming Sessions:**

The Senate will reconvene Monday, March 2 at 3 p.m. to resume consideration of the motion to proceed on H.R. 6644, the Housing for the 21st Century Act, with a cloture vote scheduled for 5:30 p.m. The House will meet in pro forma session at 9 a.m. Monday.
