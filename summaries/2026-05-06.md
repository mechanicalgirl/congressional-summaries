# Congressional Summary (run on 2026-05-06)

[https://www.congress.gov/congressional-record/volume-172/issue-77/daily-digest](https://www.congress.gov/congressional-record/volume-172/issue-77/daily-digest)

The Senate convened in pro forma session on Monday, May 4, 2026, and adjourned until Thursday, May 7, while the House met briefly the same day to introduce 19 public bills and 11 resolutions before also adjourning until Thursday, May 7. Neither chamber held committee meetings or hearings during this period. The House approved several committee reports covering appropriations for agriculture and rural development, veterans' benefits, court monitor appointments, bail reform, and small business regulatory transparency. Multiple House members submitted extensions of remarks to recognize constituent achievements and community leaders, including recognition of a Dallas middle school's educational visit, a World War II veteran's burial ceremony, and a New Jersey robotics team's accomplishments. The Senate received eight bills that had passed the House and held them at the desk for future action.
